## MOTION PROMPTS

### SHOT 01 — HANDS / PLEATED TROUSERS
**Model:** Seedance 2.0
**Duration:** 5–6 seconds
**Camera:** Locked. Zero movement.

**Prompt:**
Camera locked throughout. The right hand makes one final deliberate adjustment — thumb presses gently against the waistband fabric, fingers tighten briefly, then release and settle. The heavy wool fabric holds its crease. The brass buckle catches the raking light for a moment as the hand withdraws. Everything returns to absolute stillness. Light holds. Shadow holds.

**Negative prompt:** Distorted fingers, morphing hands, extra limbs, face appearing, camera movement, any element not in original frame, overexposed highlights, unnatural skin tone.

---

### SHOT 02 — FULL LOOK / T-SHIRT + TROUSERS
**Model:** Seedance 2.0
**Duration:** 6–8 seconds
**Camera:** Locked. Zero movement.

**Prompt:**
Camera locked throughout. He breathes once — chest rises barely perceptibly beneath the black T-shirt, holds a beat, then falls. His weight shifts almost imperceptibly onto his left foot — a millimeter of movement, nothing more. The shadow on the right side of his body deepens slightly as he exhales. He returns to complete stillness. Light direction does not change.

**Negative prompt:** Walking, gesturing, head turning, face warp, morphing features, camera movement, smile, text overlays, shaky motion, overexposed highlights.

---

### SHOT 03 — FACE / IDENTITY
**Model:** Kling 3.0
**Duration:** 5–6 seconds
**Camera:** Locked. Zero movement.

**Prompt:**
Camera locked. One slow breath — chest rises almost imperceptibly beneath the white crew neck, holds for two counts, releases. One natural blink, unhurried. Eyes remain in their downward gaze throughout. Nothing else moves. The warm shadow on the right side of his face holds. The hard light on the left side holds.

**Negative prompt:** Distorted face, morphing features, extra limbs, camera movement, smile, head turn, eyes turning to camera, plastic skin, overexposed highlights, warping neck or jaw.

---

### SHOT 04 — WALKING / ARCHITECTURE
**Model:** Seedance 2.0
**Duration:** 8–10 seconds
**Camera:** Locked on tripod throughout. Zero movement.

**Prompt:**
Camera locked on tripod — does not move at any point. He continues his slow deliberate walk deeper into the space, taking 4–5 unhurried steps away from camera. His shadow extends and shifts on the marble floor as he moves. His silhouette becomes slightly smaller as he recedes toward the far wall. The window light from the left holds constant. The floor reflects his movement beneath him.

**Negative prompt:** Camera shake, handheld motion, the man turning around, pace increase, additional people, furniture appearing, abrupt cuts, morphing walls or floor.

---

### SHOT 05 — DETAIL / LOAFERS
**Model:** Seedance 2.0
**Duration:** 5–6 seconds
**Camera:** Locked at floor level. Zero movement.

**Prompt:**
Camera locked at floor level throughout. The right foot lifts — a single slow deliberate step forward. The polished leather sole peels cleanly from the marble, the shoe rises, moves forward, sets down. The trouser hem swings once with the movement and settles. The raking shadow shifts across the floor surface as the foot moves, then stills. One step. Then stillness.

**Negative prompt:** Both feet moving at once, rushing pace, camera movement, face appearing, additional objects entering frame, blur artifacts, extra limbs appearing.

---

### SHOT 06 — HERO / THE ARCH
**Model:** Kling 3.0 4K
**Duration:** 8–10 seconds
**Camera:** Very slow dolly backward — 2 feet over 8 seconds.

**Prompt:**
Camera dolls backward very slowly — 2 feet over 8 seconds, constant and smooth. The archway grows slightly wider in the frame as the camera retreats. The man remains perfectly centered and completely still — arms at sides, no movement. The warm amber edge light holds on his silhouette throughout. The dark stone mass of the arch deepens around him. He does not move. The exterior light beyond the arch holds.

**Negative prompt:** Man turning, man walking, camera pan, camera tilt, handheld shake, additional figures appearing, text overlays, exterior light blowing out, morphing stone or architecture, speed ramp.
